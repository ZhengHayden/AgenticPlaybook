## The unique value we bring

**1. We size the prize before we spend a dollar.**
Most programs start with a use case someone is excited about. We start with a **top-down automation scan** across Function × Business Group that shows, on a single heatmap, where agentic automation frees the most labor capacity — grounded in regional, sector-specific benchmarks. You see the whole landscape before you commit to a corner of it.

**2. We replace opinion with a defensible portfolio.**
Candidates are scored on value, automation suitability, agentic fit, and risk — then triaged through a 2×2 funnel into a ranked portfolio. The output is a prioritized roadmap a CFO can challenge and a sponsor can sign.

**3. We make "good agent design" something a team can actually do.**
Agentic architecture becomes a structured classification exercise built on a proven vocabulary — **5 agent archetypes, 3 human-interaction modes, and 6 orchestration patterns** — each with explicit selection criteria and a complexity gate that prevents over-engineering. Risk and human-in-the-loop checkpoints are designed in, not bolted on.

**4. We never lose the trail.**
Every AI suggestion is persisted with its rationale; every gate decision links to its supporting evidence; every deliverable is exported as PDF, Markdown, or spreadsheet. The entire transformation is auditable end to end — the difference between a program a regulator trusts and one they don't.

**5. We give the organization a living library, not a one-time report.**
A sector-based **Agentic Use Case Library** — spanning industries from Energy & Utilities to Electronics Manufacturing — packages real, deep-dive reference cases with attached, downloadable, previewable artifacts. Intelligence, design, and validation in one place, compounding with every engagement.
